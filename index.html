The various domains in which you can apply are:

  • Corporate : Welcome to the Corporate Domain, a group of enthusiastic people who work on various levels ranging from organising events, Human Resource Management, arranging sponsors, and lastly ensuring a strong bond is made between the audience and the organisation. We bring our Alumni community together and plan events accordingly for DAA And lastly ensuring a strong bond is maintained between the aspirants and the organization. Join us if you wish to dive in and level up your game in the world of corporate.
  • Operation : From planning to implementation, all the aspects of conducting an event are managed by the operations. They look behind the scenes at each and every minute detail. At DAA, we collaborate with our alumni community together and plan events on a professional level. If you are curious to develop and learn more about the professional world. Join us now!
  • VFX and Graphics : The VFX team of the Creatives domain carefully curates and caters to the needs of video production and editing for social media such as the one you are viewing.
  • Content Writing : Welcome to the world of content creation. We're a group of passionate writers who put thoughts into words through brochures, blogs, reports and social media content like this reel! If u want to discover ways to generate engaging content that has a significant impact, then this is the place for you.
  • Cultural : The performing arts range from vocal and instrumental music, dance to pantomime, sung verse and beyond. Numerous cultural manifestations that showcase human inventiveness and are, to some extent, present in many other intangible cultural heritage areas are included in them. If you have a passion for both dancing and music. You're in the right spot.
  • Emceeing : Now let me tell you about the emceeing domain. We host a variety of events, directly interact with the Alumni and we manage the flow of the event. If you're a person with a passion for delivering spectacular events, this is the perfect place for you.
  • Graphic Designing : The Graphics Designing team of the creatives domain is a team of craftsmen who create powerful visual content for a variety of digital channels, such as social media platforms. If you enjoy making designs and posters, this is your place to learn and excel in the fields of graphic design.
  • Photography : “Photography is an austere and blazing poetry of the real.” If you are passionate about photographing and making art. Welcome to the photography domain, where you will learn how to use your lens to create team tales.
